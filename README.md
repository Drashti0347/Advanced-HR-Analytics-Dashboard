# 👥 Advanced HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

Organizations generate vast amounts of employee data related to recruitment, workforce demographics, employee performance, compensation, satisfaction, and attrition. Traditional HR reports often lack interactivity and fail to provide actionable insights for strategic workforce planning.

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Power BI**, transforming raw HR data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard enables HR professionals and management to monitor workforce trends, employee performance, recruitment effectiveness, salary distribution, and employee retention through dynamic and interactive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

✔ Monitor overall workforce performance and organizational health.

✔ Analyze employee demographics and workforce distribution.

✔ Track recruitment sources and hiring trends.

✔ Evaluate employee performance and satisfaction.

✔ Analyze compensation and workforce retention.

✔ Enable data-driven HR decision-making through interactive dashboards.

# 💡 Key Insights

📌 Production department has the highest workforce among all departments.

📌 Employee attrition rate is approximately **33%**, highlighting opportunities to improve employee retention.

📌 The majority of employees belong to the **36–45 age group**, representing the organization's core workforce.

📌 Indeed and LinkedIn are the most effective recruitment channels.

📌 Software Engineering records the highest employee satisfaction scores.

📌 Executive Office employees receive the highest average salaries.

📌 Most employees are rated **"Fully Meets"**, indicating strong overall workforce performance.

📌 Active employees account for the largest proportion of the workforce.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

👥 Strengthen employee retention programs to reduce attrition.

📈 Invest more in high-performing recruitment channels such as Indeed and LinkedIn.

💰 Monitor salary structures to ensure internal equity across departments.

⭐ Recognize and reward high-performing employees to improve engagement.

📊 Track employee satisfaction regularly to improve workplace culture.

🎯 Utilize Employee 360° insights during performance reviews and talent development.
